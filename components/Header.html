Our Founder

Dr. Waqas A. Khan - CEO / Founder

Dr. Waqas A. Khan holds a PhD in Comparative Education from the University of Hiroshima, Japan, where he focused on comparative educational assessment of Japan and Pakistan under the prestigious JSPS Ronpaku Fellowship. He also holds an M.Phil/MS in Quality Management, along with an MBA, B.Ed, M.Ed, and LLB from renowned institutions in Pakistan. Additionally, Dr. Khan earned a diploma in Practical Project Management from Ateneo De Manila University, Philippines, and holds the ISO 9001:2008 Lead Auditor Certification from the Singapore Quality Institute International (SQII).

Dr. Khanâ€™s career is marked by distinguished fellowships and leadership roles. He is a Fellow of the International Centre for Journalists (ICFJ), Washington, DC, where he worked with leading platforms such as Voice of America, USA Today, and Florida Times. In Pakistan, he contributes regularly to publications such as Pakistan Today and The News. He is also a Fellow of the International Academy of Leadership (IAF) in Germany. Dr. Khan collaborated with the Munich Chamber of Commerce and various German organizations to foster leadership development among emerging political professionals.

With over a decade of academic experience, Dr. Khan has taught Educational Leadership and Management, Advanced Assessment, and Civic Education at esteemed institutions, including UMT Lahore, FC College, and Government College Lahore. Currently, he serves as the Director of Hazara Public School & College, Changa Manga, an institution founded by his mother in 1985 to provide quality education to underprivileged children. Under his visionary leadership, the school has grown to educate over 7,000 students.
